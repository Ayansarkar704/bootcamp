About the Bootcamp

Our bootcamp is designed for individuals who want to break into the tech industry. Whether you're a beginner or have some experience, our program will teach you the skills you need to become a full-stack web developer.

Curriculum

  • Week 1-2: HTML, CSS, and Responsive Design
  • Week 3-4: JavaScript and DOM Manipulation
  • Week 5-6: Front-end Frameworks (React)
  • Week 7-8: Back-end Development (Node.js, Express)
  • Week 9-10: Databases (MongoDB, SQL)
  • Week 11: Final Project & Deployment

Bootcamp Schedule

Our bootcamp runs in the following format:

  • Duration: 12 Weeks
  • Format: Full-Time or Part-Time
  • Start Dates: January 15th, April 20th, July 10th
  • Location: Online or In-Person (Location: 123 Tech St, City)
